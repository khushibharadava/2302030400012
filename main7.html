Our Management Team

At Collaborative art gallery, our leadership team is dedicated to fostering a vibrant, inclusive space for artists and art enthusiasts. With expertise in curation, operations, and marketing, we work to bring compelling exhibitions, engaging events, and meaningful artistic collaborations to life.

1. Isabella Hart – Founder & Director

A passionate advocate for contemporary art, Isabella founded Aurora Art Collective in 2018 with the vision of creating a space where artists could collaborate and showcase their work. With over 15 years of experience in arts management, she has curated exhibitions in London, New York, and Tokyo. Under her leadership, the gallery has grown into a thriving hub for creativity.

2. Marco Ferrara – Head Curator

Marco is an award-winning curator specializing in modern and experimental art. He has worked with leading galleries and biennales across Europe. His keen eye for emerging talent and innovative exhibitions helps shape the artistic vision of Aurora Art Collective.

3. Sophia Nguyen – Operations & Events Manager

With a background in event planning and cultural programming, Sophia ensures that the gallery runs smoothly. From coordinating artist residencies to organizing large-scale art festivals, she plays a crucial role in delivering impactful exhibitions and engaging community events.

4. Daniel Cohen – Marketing & Community Engagement

Daniel leads the gallery’s marketing efforts, building strong relationships with artists, collectors, and media outlets. Through social media campaigns, press outreach, and strategic partnerships, he ensures that Aurora Art Collective remains a key player in the contemporary art scene.

5. Aria Patel – Artist Liaison & Gallery Coordinator

Aria works closely with artists, handling exhibition logistics, residency programs, and installations. With a background in fine arts and arts administration, she ensures that every artist at Aurora Art Collective receives the support they need to bring their creative vision to life.
